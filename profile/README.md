**Corel WordPerfect Office Suite** is a professional productivity solution trusted by legal firms, government agencies, and business professionals worldwide. As a powerful **Corel WordPerfect Word Processor**, it features Reveal Codes technology for granular formatting control, plus built-in legal tools including pleading wizard, line numbering, document compare, and redaction support.
